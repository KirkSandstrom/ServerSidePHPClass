Bio

I decided to take this course to continue building upon my web development skills. I have already taken a variety of previous web development courses at UNH, including Client-Side web development. By learning about the server-side aspect of web development, I hope to gain a greater understanding of the "full stack" behind a web-page or web-app.

Although I have previously taken a few other courses regarding web development and design at UNH, I still consider myself to have little real-world experience. This past summer I developed a new website for the family business, however I used the WordPress CMS to do so and as a result didn't end up doing much coding. Still, the experience provided me with plenty of valuable in-sight into site design, layout, and usability.

The programmer's struggle: It doesn't work... Why? It works... Why?
